SpeakSwap Privacy Policy

Effective Date:

1. Introduction

Welcome to SpeakSwap! Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your personal data while using our real-time language translation services.

2. Information We Collect

  • Personal Information: When you sign up or use SpeakSwap, we may collect your name, email, and contact details.
  • Usage Data: We collect anonymous data on how the app is used to improve performance and user experience.
  • Audio Data: Your voice inputs are processed in real-time for translation but are not stored or shared.
  • Device Information: We may collect basic device information such as OS type and app version to ensure compatibility.

3. How We Use Your Data

  • To provide real-time speech translation services.
  • To improve app functionality and user experience.
  • To enhance language model accuracy and AI-driven responses.
  • To comply with legal requirements or respond to lawful requests.

4. Data Security & Protection

  • SpeakSwap uses end-to-end encryption to secure data transmission.
  • We do not store or sell your audio data.
  • Access to your information is limited to authorized personnel only.

5. Third-Party Services

  • SpeakSwap integrates with Google Cloud Speech-to-Text and Translate API for real-time translations.
  • Third-party services may process data only for translation purposes and follow their respective privacy policies.

6. User Rights & Control

  • You can request data deletion by contacting our support team.
  • You can disable microphone access at any time from your device settings.
  • Users can opt out of analytics tracking through the app settings.

7. Changes to This Policy

  • We may update this policy from time to time to reflect changes in our services.
  • Users will be notified of major updates through the app or via email.

8. Contact Us

For any privacy-related questions or concerns, please contact us at support@speakswap.com.

SpeakSwap is committed to protecting your data and ensuring a secure translation experience. By using our services, you agree to the terms outlined in this Privacy Policy.
